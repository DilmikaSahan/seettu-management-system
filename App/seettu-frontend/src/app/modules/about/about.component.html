What is Seettu?

Seettu is a traditional rotating savings and credit association (ROSCA) where a group of people contribute a fixed amount regularly to a common fund. Each member takes turns receiving the total collection, helping them save money and access funds when needed.

Seettu Potha brings this age-old practice into the digital era, making it more organized, transparent, and secure for modern communities.
